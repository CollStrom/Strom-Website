Upstream Oil & Gas

Procurement Services

We provide equipment and material procurement services to the oil and gas industry. Procurement in the Oil & Gas Industry has become an essential service in today’s competitive marketplace. Our role is to find and supply all the manufactured goods associated with the exploration and delivery of oil and gas. Our customers expect an exceptional level of service and we fulfil that expectation by delivering a reliable, honest, diligent and conscientious service that meets their specific demands.

Products and services include:

  • Valves and Accessories, Pipes/tubes/hoses and fittings, Drilling Consumables, Compressors, Pumps, Gauges, Heat Exchangers, Pressurization Tools, Wellhead materials, OCTGs etc…
  • Safety, Protection and Fire Fighting equipment.
  • Turnkey Pipeline procurement.

Maintenace and Engineering Services

Strom Global Services is responsible for providing maintenance , repairs and operations services to the oil and Gas industry. Our capability is based on total support services fortified with experience and competence built in our human capacity. Our Engineers are experienced and will maintain and service all equipment installed on our clients facilities at all times.

Services include:

  • Engineering Design
  • Turnkey Pipeline Construction
  • Fabrication and Calibration Services
  • Construction Management and Supervision Services
  • Bunker construction, Gas trains, Electrical, processing facilities, Pipeline Laying and Repairs
  • Drilling, Dredging, Pipelines, Heavy Duty Procurement
