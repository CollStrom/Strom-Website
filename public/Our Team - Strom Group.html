Management Team


Bobby Adolphus Igenegba

Founder/CEO

Bobby, a graduate of Philosophy from the University of Benin, Nigeria is the Founder/CEO of Strom Global services. He is a certified member of Chartered Institute of Management (CIM) and Chartered Institute of Personnel Management (CIPM) with over twenty years of experience in the Oil and Gas industry and the Power sector. Bobby has previously worked for Pipeline Production and Marketing Company (PPMC) and Avidor Oil and Gas where, he successfully designed and implemented new marketing strategies that grew the business from a cumulative turnover of N50million to $500m within four years as Business Development Manager.

As Founder and CEO of Strom, Bobby has driven the implementation of Company policies with a primary focus on growth, profits and increasing shareholder return. His conceptual ability and result-oriented approach has powered the Company from start up to recording impressive turnovers and other achievements. He is also a pioneer Director at METSCO Africa Energy Services, a Nigerian-Canadian Energy service provider.


Dr Chike Chukwudi Onyejekwe

Non-Executive Chairman

A Geologist by background with extensive depth and breadth of experience in the oil and gas industry spanning over 35 years in various working, leadership and senior management positions within and outside Nigeria, covering Exploration, Development, Asset management, Operations and Management having attended the Ashridge Business School London and Wharton School of Business, University of Pennsylvania USA

In 2008, he became the Managing Director of Shell Nigeria Exploration and Production Company (SNEPCo), a position he held for 6 years. Chike retired from SNEPCo in 2014 and took up another job, the position of pioneer GMD of Aiteo Eastern Exploration and Production Company (AEEPCo), the largest Upstream Nigerian Indigenous Oil and Gas company in terms of resource base, size and production, following divestment of that Oil and Gas Block by SHELL. In his capacity as the pioneer GMD of Aiteo, he grew the newly acquired Oil and gas block from a 23,000 b/d production prior to divestment and operatorship handover to Aiteo E & P to 95,000 b/d production in one year; a feat that was applauded within the industry and by Government (NNPC).

In 2016, The Anambra State University found him a worthy professional and outstanding Leader and was one of the Three people awarded a Doctor of Science (D.Sc) by the University Senate.

Chike retired from Aiteo on June 1 2019 after his 5 years contract as GMD, and became the Non-Executive Chairman of Strom in 2020.
