Bulk importation of Products

We import large volume of petroleum products to serve varied needs. Our products are sourced from tursted and quality certified sources who uphold the highest standard of quality control and quality assurance. We undergo a through process from purchase to delivery of product which include independent lab tests and Q&Q; tests to ensure product quality. Products we import include; Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK), Premium Motor Spirit (PMS). Automotive Gas Oil (AGO), Low Pour Fuel Oil (LPFO) and Aviation Fuel (JET A1).

Strom Global services also handles sourcing, shipping and sales of petroleum products on behalf of clients. We have an excellent team with over 25 years combined experience in the downstream sector. We ensure a smooth and transparent process from start to finish

Calibration and Haulage Services

We service our clients by recalibrate all storage devices which is a statutory requirement by the Department of Petroleum Resources of Nigeria that all storage tanks and facilities should be calibrated at least once a year. Recalibrating of storage facilities has been demonstrated to save considerable costs on current diesel expenditure because the process identifies and verifies the actual quantity of product storage capacity because materials used in storage facilities undergo expansion in capacity after a few months of constant contact with petroleum products and access to moisture and this increases the quantity of storage without noticeable physical appearances as this will provide room for sharp practices from the current diesel suppliers.

This service will be proposed to be conducted annually and will assist to provide accurate cost expense forecasting to drive adequate planning outlay for the our clients.
